Chelsea boss, Thomas Tuchel, has named the two Chelsea players he is happy with following the Blues’ away Premier League victory against Leicester City on Saturday afternoon. According to Tuchel, he is happy with Christian Pulisic and Hakim Ziyech after Chelsea defeated Leicester at the King Power Stadium. Goals from Antonio Rudiger, N’Golo Kante and Pulisic gave Chelsea all three points against the Foxes. The result against Leicester means Chelsea are on top of the Premier League table with 26 points from twelve matches. “I’m very happy that, after a long time when he [Christian Pulisic] suffered from Coronavirus and injury, that he is back and decisive straight away and I’m very happy also for Hakim, who deserved to start, that he was again involved with an assist,” Tuchel was quoted by Chelsea’s official website as saying after the match. Chelsea will now take on Juventus in the Champions League on Tuesday at Stamford Bridge.
